Finance FAQ

Common questions.

Can I get finance with a low credit score?

We work with multiple lenders, so even if you’ve been declined elsewhere, get in touch — we’ll do what we can to find an option that works for you.

Can I use a trade-in as my deposit?

Yes — bring your current vehicle in for a valuation and we’ll put that value straight towards your deposit.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are processed within 24–48 hours, depending on the lender and documentation provided.

What documents do I need?

Typically your ID, proof of residence, payslips and bank statements. We’ll confirm exactly what’s needed once you apply.
